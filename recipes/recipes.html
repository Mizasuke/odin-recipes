Istrian pasta with truffles

Description

Favorite dish of Istrian people. You can do it too, now.

Ingredients for 2 persons

  • 250g pasta (pljukanci, fu≈æi)
  • 50 g butter
  • 200/250 ml cooking cream
  • 80g truffles

You can add per your wishes

  • 30g of parmesan
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. Put pasta in pot. Pasta needs to be pljukanci or fuzi.
  2. In time pasta cooks itself, you need to prepare sauce
    1. Put butter in pan
    2. When butter gets wattery, put truffle sauce in it.
    3. Add cooking cream and mix it.
    4. Add spices, salt and pepper. You can add your favorite spice, too.
    5. Cook it on low fire
  3. When pasta is done, strain pasta to get water out. Put pasta in pan with sauce.
    Cook it for one more minute so it connects every ingredient.
  4. If you want to add parmesan do it now and you can now put it in front of people and be proud of yourself.
